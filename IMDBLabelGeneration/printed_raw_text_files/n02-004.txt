To Beryl, at least, this was anti-climax with a vengeance. She had expected to be met
with violent reproaches - and here he was bland and smiling. But it did not take her
long to realise that his mood was less pleasant than appeared on the surface. He told
her nonchalantly, as he led the way to his own veranda, that he had taken the liberty
of asking Jack - as a representative of the Vallin family - to come over and make the
presentation in her stead.
