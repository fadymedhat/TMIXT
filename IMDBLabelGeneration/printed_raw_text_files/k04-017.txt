'Are you enjoying yourself, Olimpia?' she asked idly, tapping the girl's cheek lightly
with her fan, in passing; but she did not wait for the answer. Disturbed by vague
uneasiness, she was wondering whether Olimpia had been watching her talk with
Orsini. Surely she could not have overheard anything they said? A moment's reflec-
tion reassured her on that point, for she was certain no one had been standing near
them.
