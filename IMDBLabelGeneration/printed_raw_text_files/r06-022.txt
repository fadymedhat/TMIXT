This was strange, even magic, because the owner's name was Pierre Tortorolo which,
in Nicoison Italian means 'turtledove'. Pierre Turtledove. When we woke up properly
it was raining, an even more hopeless rain than London, and we looked out of the
windows at the weeping trees and the curling white breath of the mountains. The
land looked like a beaten woman and the turtle-doves cried her shame.
