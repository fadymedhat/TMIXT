They objected to the word Absolution. So the phrase 'or the remission of sins' was
added. But Absolution remained. They objected to the word Confirmation. So the
phrase 'or the laying on of hands upon children baptized and able to render an account
of their faith according to the Catechism following' were added. But Confirmation
remained. Other changes in the Book were equally minor except for the new section
on the Sacraments added to the Catechism.
