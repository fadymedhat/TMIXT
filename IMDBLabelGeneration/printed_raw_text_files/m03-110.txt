Sally and of course Mrs Septimus, for surely Mr Septimus stood in the same case

be inopportune; and with the idea came a somewhat vulgar impulse, which I refused,
to watch Mr Septimus more closely, Mrs Caine too and Mr Gilberto who would surely
show some anxiety by now if he noticed anything untoward.
