"Good-bye, Dai," said Morfydd, smiling from the bottom step. "Good-bye, Morfydd."
He sought to enfold her in his arms. But he was too late. She was already up the
bus steps, and the bus was away. And Morfydd Owen waving, unkissed, from the
window. And Dai, on the pavement, knowing in his heart that Morfydd was leaving
him as surely as she was leaving Dinas.
