Thus had they parted the previous evening and now Diana was trailing up the grav-
elled drive to the hospital alone. Of course one couldn't say for certain when a doctor
would be free during the day; tea was served from four until five-thirty in the residents'
common-room, which proved the elasticity of medical commitments. Something had
cropped up which required Nigel's attention, she was convinced, or he would have
granted her small request to be met at the gates.
